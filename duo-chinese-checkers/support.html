Gameplay

Select one of your marbles, then choose a highlighted destination. You can move to an adjacent open spot or jump over neighboring marbles when a landing spot is open.

Computer mode includes three skill levels. Two-player mode lets two people play together on the same device.

Platforms

Duo Chinese Checkers supports iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV, and Apple Vision Pro.
