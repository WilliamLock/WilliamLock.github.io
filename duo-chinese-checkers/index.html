About

Duo Chinese Checkers is a simplified, focused take on the classic marble-jumping strategy game. Play head-to-head with another person or challenge the computer across three skill levels.

The board is designed for fast reading, smooth move selection, and a refined tabletop feel across iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV, and Apple Vision Pro.

Classic Moves

Move to adjacent spaces or chain jumps across the board to reach the opposite side.

Computer Play

Choose from three skill levels when you want a quick solo match.

Big Screen Ready

Landscape layouts and focus controls keep the game readable on TV and Vision.
