Gameplay

Select four colors to make a guess. Black pins mean a color is correct and in the right position. White pins mean a color is correct but in a different position. Solve the hidden code within ten rows.

Platforms

Codebreaker Classic supports iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV, and Apple Vision Pro.
